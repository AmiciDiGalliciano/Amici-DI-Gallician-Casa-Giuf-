Privacy Policy

Welcome to Amici Di Gallicianò. This Privacy Policy is intended to describe how this website is managed.

1. Personal Data

This is a static website (HTML/CSS/JS). We do not collect users' personal data, there is no registration, and there are no online payments.

2. Geolocation

The site offers a map feature that may request the user's location ("Use my location"). This data is processed exclusively on your browser (client-side) to show your position on the map via Leaflet.js and OpenStreetMap. No location data is sent to external servers owned by us or saved.

3. Voluntary Contacts

If you choose to click on the "Call" or "WhatsApp" buttons, the sending of the message or the call occurs through third-party apps (WhatsApp, Phone app) pre-installed on your device. Any data you decide to send us via chat or phone will be processed solely to respond to your request for information or booking.

4. Third-Party Services

The site integrates links to Google Maps and Instagram profiles. Clicking on these links opens external services subject to their own privacy policies.

5. Cookies

The site exclusively uses the browser's `sessionStorage` for a single technical purpose: to remember if you have already viewed the logo animation during the current session, so as not to show it again at every page change. We do not use profiling or advertising tracking cookies.
