The dining room

A simple, concrete, village table

No fake scenery is needed here: tables, memorabilia, tablecloths, bottles, bread, and people are enough. The dining room tells exactly what tourists look for when arriving in such a village: authenticity without theater.

How to recognize it

Three real references to arrive without wandering around

These photos are not decorative: they serve to recognize the right spot when you are already in Gallicianò. First look at the facade, then the sign near the church, then the engraving on the pavement. Yes, finally practical orientation, not a treasure hunt with growing hunger.
