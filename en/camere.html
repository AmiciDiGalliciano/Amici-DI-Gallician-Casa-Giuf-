Practical tips before arrival

Arrive prepared, enjoy your stay better

Casa Giufà is located in the village of Gallicianò: an authentic, quiet place far from easily accessible services. A little organization before arriving makes the stay simpler and more peaceful.

01

Stop at the supermarket first

Before arriving in Gallicianò, we recommend buying water, snacks, personal products, any medicines and whatever may be needed for children, elderly people or guests with special needs.

02

Communicate allergies and needs to Elisa

In case of allergies, food intolerances, specific requests or special needs, inform Elisa before your arrival. She will gather useful information and communicate it to Trattoria Nizio Paleò when necessary.

03

Bring what makes you feel good

A sweatshirt for the evening, comfortable shoes, a water bottle and small personal products help you to better enjoy the village, the trails and the slow pace of Gallicianò.

Simple goal: to allow every guest to have a good stay, without unnecessary surprises and with the right information before departure.

Useful information before booking

How many guests can it accommodate?

Casa Giufà can accommodate up to 6 people in total. There are two rooms on two separate floors: each room has 1 double bed, 1 single bed, an independent entrance and can accommodate a maximum of 3 people.

Can I book just one floor?

Yes. The two floors are separate and have independent entrances. You can book a single independent room, with a maximum of 3 guests per room, or the entire house for up to 6 guests.

Are there bathrooms?

Yes, each floor has its own private bathroom.

Where is it located and where can I park?

It is located in the ancient village of Gallicianò. Recommended arrival on Maps: Parking, 89030 Gallicianò RC. After parking, Casa Giufà / reception area can be reached on foot in about 90-100 meters towards Via Anaghorio, 89030 Gallicianò RC.

How do I book?

Contact Elisa directly via WhatsApp or phone to check availability. Rooms start from €65.

Breakfast and meals

Breakfast is not directly included in the room, but it can be organized at the facilities in the village. For lunch and dinner, we will recommend the best places to taste traditional local cuisine, such as Trattoria Nizio Paleò. For those staying at Casa Giufà, the complete agreed menu costs €25 per person instead of €30, subject to confirmation.

How far in advance should I book?

We require a minimum notice of 2 days to best welcome you and prepare the rooms.
