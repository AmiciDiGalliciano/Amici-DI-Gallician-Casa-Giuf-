Real, clear, downloadable trails

Here you don’t need buttons placed for decoration: every trail has a start on Maps, a downloadable track, a safety note, and a readable map. The goal is to truly help the guest prepare, without pretending these are official or guided excursions.

Important note on the trails. The tracks are real GPX provided by users and reworked into lightweight GPX/KML/GeoJSON versions. They serve to orient yourself, prepare the visit, and follow your own point on the local map. They do not replace signage, caution, weather, physical conditions, on-site checks, or updated information. Google Maps is useful for reaching the start/finish; the precise track must be followed via GPX/KML or the local map.
Note: In case of orientation problems or signal loss, we highly recommend consulting the Wikiloc app to find local tracks of Gallicianò.

Trails map

Real map with GPS

Choose a trail, activate your location and the site shows you how close you are to the track. Works best on mobile, with authorized GPS and connection. For serious offline use, it is better to import the GPX into Wikiloc or a hiking app.

Preparation

Advice before leaving

To best experience the trails we recommend suitable shoes, water, a hat on hot days, downloaded GPX before leaving, and a brief check of weather conditions. For longer stretches, it is always recommended to check with Giuseppe or Elisa.

Recommended equipment

  • • Trekking shoes with treaded soles
  • • Flask with enough water
  • • Hat and sun protection
  • • Comfortable and layered clothing

Orientation and Safety

We advise you to consult the trail description before leaving and write to Giuseppe to receive practical information. Do not leave without checking weather, water, trail terrain, and personal conditions.

Recommended arrival

Arrive at the parking lot, then 90-100 meters on foot

To avoid confusion inside the village, set your navigator to Parcheggio, 89030 Gallicianò RC: the first button automatically uses your current position as the start. After leaving the car, continue on foot towards Via Anaghorio, 89030 Gallicianò RC: the distance is short, about 90-100 meters. Do not drive all the way to the front of the house.
