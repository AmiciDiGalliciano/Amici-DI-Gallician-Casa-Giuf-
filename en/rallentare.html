Find your own time

Gallicianò is the place to truly slow down: fewer screens, less noise, more nature, silence, and presence.

Time changes pace here

Slowing down doesn't mean stopping

In Gallicianò, time doesn't rush. And today, this is almost a form of luxury: less noise, fewer stimuli, more attention to what you are truly experiencing.

We are used to always living with something to check: notifications, messages, screens, appointments, noise. The mind stays on even when the body just wants to rest. Arriving at Casa Giufà doesn't just mean changing the scenery: it means changing the rhythm.

Here, silence is not empty. It's space. Walking among the stones of the village, feeling the wind, looking at the fiumara from above, listening to footsteps in the narrow streets, and waking up without the continuous noise of the city help you become present again.

Slowing down works because it restores attention: when you are not forced to jump from one stimulus to another, you start noticing details that usually disappear. The scent of the earth, the light on the houses, the smell of cheeses, the bread, the simple cooking, the sound of animals.

Intentional choice

No television. No Wi-Fi. More presence.

At Casa Giufà, this is not a lack: it is part of the experience. An invitation to sleep better, talk more, walk unhurriedly, look out the window, and sit without having to do something else immediately.

01

Fewer stimuli, more presence

When screens, notifications, and noise decrease, attention stops jumping from one thing to another. The place becomes more legible, more intense, more memorable.

02

Silence lightens

The silence of the village doesn't turn off the mind: it gives it a break. It's that rare pause when the body understands it doesn't have to always be on alert.

03

Walking rearranges thoughts

The slow pace among alleys, stone, and nature helps shape thoughts. You don't need to do big things: often, it's enough to move unhurriedly.

04

Smells and tastes make memories

The earth, the cheeses, the bread, the wine, the local cuisine, and the air of the fiumara remain imprinted because they speak to the senses even before words.

Gallicianò teaches this: not everything of value has to be fast. Some things need time to be understood. An ancient house. A silent village. A fiumara. A family history. An encounter.

The rhythms of the village

A day at Casa Giufà

The Morning

Waking up is accompanied by the light rising over the Aspromonte hills. The morning can start with local village products, when organized before arrival, before tying your boots and taking the path towards the Fiumara Amendolea or simply taking a stroll through the cobbled alleys of the village.

The Lunch

After the walk, the food. Upon request, you can stop for an authentic lunch at Trattoria Nizio Paleò or at other hospitable houses: homemade bread, strong cheeses, local meat, and wine that tastes of this land, strictly genuine.

The Afternoon

The afternoon hours are made for sitting outside, reading a book in the sun, exploring the small Orthodox Church, and visiting the Fountain of Love, where Greek history is breathed in every corner.

The Evening and Night

When the occasional tourists leave, the village shuts down in total quiet. Returning to Casa Giufà means finding the warmth of a real stone house. Outside the window, only the perfect darkness of the Aspromonte, ideal for admiring an uncontaminated starry sky before a deep and uninterrupted sleep.
